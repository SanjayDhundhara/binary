5 Things You Should Know About Unstoppable Domains

1
Founders of Unstoppable Domains
Unstoppable Domains is a San-Francisco based company founded in 2018 by Matthew Gould, Braden Pezeshki, Bogdan Gusiev, and Bradley Kam. They are one of the earliest recipients of our ZILHive Grants.
2
A naming registration service
They're building a naming registration service to replace long hexadecimal account addresses with simple human-readable names.
3
Making it easier for the non-blockchain crowd
Aimed at wallets and exchanges, this project is critical to make it easier for the non-blockchain crowd to own blockchain-backed assets and transfer them around.
4
Unstoppable Domains brings massive traffic to Zilliqa
They also launched a new blockchain domain extension deployed on the Zilliqa blockchain, designed so that .zil domains can work to enable payments in any cryptocurrency. Unstoppable Domains brings massive traffic onto the Zilliqa smart contracts platform, and we are excited to partner with them again for a $250,000 .zil grant program.
5
The journey to enabling free speech
Zilliqa is thrilled to support them on their journey to enable free speech through the creation of uncensorable websites, and in making blockchain domains a new industry standard.
Go to Unstoppable Domains website
Unstoppable Domains in numbers
In just a year - the company has grown to include 19 team members.
In May 2019, they raised $4 million in a Series A funding round led by Draper Associates with additional support from Boost VC. Previously, Unstoppable Domains has received grants from Zilliqa and the Ethereum Foundation.


The company offers regular .zil domains for $10 and premium domains for $250.
